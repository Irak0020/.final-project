The Journey of a Starfish

Starfish are more than just sea creatures; they are symbols of resilience, transformation, and beauty. Found in oceans worldwide, these incredible echinoderms regenerate lost limbs, adapt to various marine environments, and move gracefully across the seafloor. The story of a starfish is one of survival, wonder, and mystery, much like the journey of life itself.






Fascinating Facts About Starfish

  • Starfish can regenerate lost arms, making them one of the most resilient sea creatures.
  • They have no brain or blood, but they use seawater to circulate nutrients.
  • There are over 2,000 species of starfish found in oceans around the world.
  • Some starfish can change their gender during their lifetime.
